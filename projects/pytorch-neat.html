PyTorch NEAT

A PyTorch implementation of Kenneth O. Stanley's Neuroevolution of Augmenting Topologies (NEAT) paper.

Code

Of Course! Go ahead and pick it apart on github.

Why

This past summer (2018) I dived into machine learning research and have undertaken an independent research project into catastrophic forgetting in neural networks with two partners. Part of our initial strategy requires evolving networks which requires an algorithm like NEAT. Sure, we could have used a previous implementation, but now I am very comfortable with PyTorch and know NEAT inside and out.
